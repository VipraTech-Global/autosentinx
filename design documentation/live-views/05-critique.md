# Live Views — Stage 5: Analysis / Design Critique (and what was done about it)

> Per `../00-PROCESS.md`, we acted as design critics on the **plan + foundation addendum** before any visual styling — **seven independent lenses** (IA/flow · accessibility+bilingual · RegTech credibility · brand/design-language · backend feasibility · live-demo narrative · maintainability). All seven returned **good-with-gaps** with concrete blocking issues. This document records: what's working, the **blocking themes → resolution (applied now)**, majors folded in, items deferred to the spec, and the **forks that need your decision at approval**. The critique's biggest service: it caught that the draft was anchored on **stale `../DESIGN.md §1` text that `../DECISIONS.md D-Q12` superseded**, and that the Arena's data is **prototype-only**, not canonical-engine.

## 5.1 What the critics agreed is working (keep)
- The **roll-up/down zoom continuum as one surface, three projections** ("a tier may hide detail but never invent it") is the right mental model and is stated as a foundation rule, not asserted per-screen.
- **V3 = C6 reuse** ("do not re-spec; reference it") avoids double-defining the detail screen.
- **The de-gamification reconciliation** (`00-foundation.md`) is the right shape: emoji→line-icon map, one orchestrated moment, composed re-voicing, named components.
- **Deferring V1 build while documenting its bones + the "omits, never fakes" over-abstraction guard** keeps the continuum honest about real-vs-drawn.
- **Accessibility-of-navigation named at plan stage** (ZoomControl keyboard/focus/deep-link).
- **The additive, ID-keyed maintainability rule** is coherent and mostly followed.

## 5.3 Major issues folded in now
- **Breach-point honesty (RegTech):** `pivotTurn` is an **advisory** classifier signal, not the panel ruling — captioned "pivot (in-call classifier — the panel ruled on the whole exchange)" and **decoupled**; both divergence states specced (panel FAIL with `pivotTurn=None` → no breach-point node; in-call Succeed with panel PASS → no breach claim). *(`04` V2-R4/R5)*
- **StrategyBeat = honest about the timer (RegTech):** an advance triggered by the 2-turn timer is labelled **"advanced (timer)"**, only a Comply-as-last-label is **"conceded"** (matches the engine + the `run_stream.py` fix); never narrate a timer as deliberate strategy. *(`00-foundation.md` LV-3; `03` M14)*
- **Scoreboard "held/breached" = PANEL outcome (RegTech):** the run-level posture line uses the **panel verdict**, never the advisory per-turn IntegrityStrip; the strip stays captioned advisory. *(`04` V2-R3; `03` M16)*
- **OutcomeBadge vocabulary (brand):** **HELD/BREACHED are declared composed display-aliases of PASS/FAIL** (same §5 shape channel: PASS=check ✓, FAIL=solid disc ●); running/judging are run-states, not outcomes. *(`00-foundation.md` LV-5/LV-6)*
- **Gate-delta icon (brand):** use **`git-compare`** (depicts our-verdict-vs-self-report = the BypassSignal semantics), **not** `alert-triangle` (§6 alarmist ban). *(`00-foundation.md` LV-3)*
- **Two competing reveals (demo):** reconciled — the funnel's one orchestrated moment is **Processing→Findings**; the Live View's is the **VerdictReveal**; **they never co-occur in one path** (entering the Arena to watch IS the reveal; the demo path goes Processing→Arena, not Processing→Findings-reveal→Arena). Stated in `00-foundation.md` LV-4. *(D-Q15 replay)*
- **Entry affordances are canonical edits (IA):** "Watch the duel" (C4) / "Open the duel" (C5) are **new controls on canonical screens** → added to the §F proposed-canonical-edit list. *(`04` §F)*
- **Arena replay pacing (demo):** specced as its own timeline (recon prelude → arc fill → beat → VerdictReveal) targeting **< 90s with narration** inside the 3-min budget. *(`04` §B; `03` M22)*
- **README format mis-attribution (maintainability):** the `⊃ supersedes` glyph is **not** in any canonical doc (DECISIONS.md supersedes in **prose**). Reworded the rule to "supersede in a dated note (prose), as `../DECISIONS.md` does"; kept the dated discipline. *(`README.md` §2.2)*
- **D-namespace overlap (maintainability):** `03 §dep` no longer **re-keys** canonical `D6/D7/D8` — it **references** them; only `D-LV*` live here. *(`03` §dep)*

## 5.6 The single most important change (consensus)
**Re-anchor the disclosure spine on `D-Q12` (BL1) and strip methodology (intents/phase-names) from V2 (BL2).** Four of seven lenses landed here: the continuum was sold on "the full chain unlocks at V3," but the authoritative decision made the detail screen landing-only — so the spine described disclosure the product will not produce, and the Arena was set to leak the very IP the rule protects. Runner-up: **stop calling the Arena's data "resolved" (BL9)** — it is prototype-only; the plan must say so or it promises what the engine can't yet produce, the exact failure the canonical ladder forbids.
